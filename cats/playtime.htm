Play with your cat

  • Cats love to play when they are bored! Why not see if your cat wants to play?
  • Wave its toy around. If it wants to play, it will hit or react to the toy.
  • Now just let it hit the toy and chase it around and you can move the toy and it might chase it!
  • If you don't have a toy, make one! There will be a tutorial on making your own cat toy soon!
